About Novelty

our story

One day in the late summer of 2017, an author, a business man, and a reality TV maven met in a coffee shop. The author wondered where budding, retired, or tired writers could exercise their skills for substantial income; the business man wondered how such a thing could stay afloat; the reality TV maven wondered how to make it fun. Together, they came up with the idea for Novelty-- a service for the commissioning of custom novels, which gives authors rights to the finished product, but allows customers to reap the satisfaction of receiving fiction catered specifically to their tastes.
